Scanderlous

Scanderlous is a 3rd year Ph.D candidate cover band in Music Covers based in Princeton, NJ.
Scanderlous' research interests lie at the intersection of the Jazz, Funk, Rock and Pop music styles.
Please contact us via email below for booking inquiries or other collaborations.
